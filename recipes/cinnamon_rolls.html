Brioche Cinnamon Roles

These yummy cinnamon rolls make an amazing, decadent breakfast (or desert!) Forget store-bought cinnamon rolls, these are way better!

Ingredients

  • cinnamon
  • salt
  • butter
  • brown sugar
  • yeast

Steps

  1. Knead dough on a lightly floured surface
  2. Stir the filling ingredients together
  3. Seal the filling in the roll
  4. Cook at 350 degrees
