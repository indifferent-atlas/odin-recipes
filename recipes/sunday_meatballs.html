Sunday Meatballs

Description

These meatballs are so delicous, especially when shared around a table with family. Plus, they're super easy to make! It's become like a family tradition of ours to make these every Sunday.

Ingredients

  • spaghetti
  • marinara sauce
  • beef

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Stir in the marinara sauce
  3. Add the beef
