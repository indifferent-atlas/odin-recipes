Coq au Vin with Butter Noodles

Description

This dish sounds so fancy...oh lala! In reality, it's one of my favorite things to make when I have friends and family over. It's so cozy and the perfect meal for a close-knit group

Ingredients

  • bacon
  • garlic
  • carrots
  • boneless skinless chicken breast
  • onion
  • egg noodles
  • chicken broth

Steps

  1. Cook the bacon and veggies in a large Dutch oven until fragrant
  2. Slowly stir in the wine and broth until the mixture begins to simmer
  3. Cook the egg noodles and place them in the veggie chicken mixture
  4. Stir over low heat until noodls are coated and serve.
